CHURN PREDICTION

Predicting customer churn by recognizing subtle patterns and anomalies in account activity.

50% REDUCTION IN CHURN
PROACTIVE RETENTION EFFORTS

THE CHALLENGE

Churn often happened silently. By the time a customer cancelled, it was too late to save them. We needed a way to identify at-risk customers before they made the decision to leave.

THE SOLUTION

I developed a model that identifies subtle patterns and anomalies in account activity (e.g., drop in usage, support ticket spikes) that correlate with churn. This allowed the team to intervene proactively.
